About Samsung

Samsung is a multinational conglomerate known for its diverse range of products, including smartphones, televisions, home appliances, and more. Founded in 1938 in South Korea, Samsung has grown to become a global leader in various industries.

Products

Samsung offers a wide array of products:

  • Smartphones
  • Televisions
  • Tablets
  • Home Appliances
  • Laptops

Innovation

Samsung is known for its commitment to innovation. They have pioneered advancements in various technologies, such as AMOLED displays, camera technology, and more.
